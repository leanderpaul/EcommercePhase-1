About.

Since our inception, Jute Emporium has been totally focused on building lasting relations, improving the lives of our Employees and providing a better alternative to non-reusable plastics.

Jute Emporium is the landmark of Jute Bags and jute handicrafts in Chennai. Jute Emporium is the first exclusive jute bags showroom of our country started its jute operations in the year 1986. Jute bags and cotton bags are eco-friendly and the only alternatives to plastic bags.


Jute Emporium is the marketing arm of DHARTI an NGO working for women empowerment and eco-friendliness by promoting jute crafts.


These jute bags are finely crafted by the talented women artisans trained under various projects of Government of India. Jute Emporium is manufacturing a complete range of jute bags, for marriages. Wedding bags, thamboolam bags, marriage bags, wedding return gifts jute bags whatever you call it; in wedding bags all kinds of jute bags are available in Jute emporium in ready stock.
