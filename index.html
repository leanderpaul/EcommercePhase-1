About Us

Jute Emporium is the leading jute bags
manufacturer, with an in-house production
capacity of over 1500 jute bags per day and
outside women self-help group members can
contribute more than 3000 jute bags per day.
We are there the largest manufacturers of eco-
friendly jute bags manufacturers in Tamilnadu.

Qualities of jute bags made by Jute Emporium are
outstanding. Our jute bags finish and style cannot
be compared with the jute bags available in the
market.
